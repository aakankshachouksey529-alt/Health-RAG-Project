Although the evidence for an association between lower glycaemic load and coronary heart disease incidence was rated as moderate certainty based on a statistically significant doseresponse relationship, the relationship itself was very weak. Together with the fact that most other outcomes were rated low to very low, this led to an overall certainty rating of low for glycaemic load.
