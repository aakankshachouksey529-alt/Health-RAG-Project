Overweight and obesity occur when lifestyle choices get out of balance  such as eating too much food, not getting enough physical activity, or having too much of one or more nutrients in the diet. On the other hand, if any of these items is not consumed in adequate quantities, a person can get under-nourished or get deficiency diseases such as anaemia, night blindness and so on.
