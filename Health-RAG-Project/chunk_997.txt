At the same time, in both general and specialized mental health care settings, the signs and symptoms of physical illness are often misattributed to a concurrent mental health condition in what is known as diagnostic overshadowing (117). These two factors have, for example, led to a systematic under-recognition and undertreatment of cardio- vascular conditions among people living with schizophrenia and bipolar disorder (118, 119).
