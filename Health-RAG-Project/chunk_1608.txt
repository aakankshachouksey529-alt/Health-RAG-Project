The campaign has more than 160 community and organizational partners and collaborators in Western Australia and has also been adapted and adopted in other parts of the country as well as overseas, in Denmark, Faroe Islands, Norway and the United States of America. By 2018, 82% of Western Australians were aware of the A-B-C campaigns, 16% of whom had taken action to improve their mental health as a result.
