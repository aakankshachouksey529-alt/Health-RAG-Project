The clear water can be further treated with solar disinfection or through chlorination. Chlorination: This is the process of adding chlorine in liquid or tablet form to drinking water stored in a protected container. Chlorine is available in a variety of sources, including solid calcium hypochlorite, liquid sodium hypochlorite or NaDCC tablets.
