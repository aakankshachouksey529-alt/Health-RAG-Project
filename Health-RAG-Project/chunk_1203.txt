BOX 4.2 Research to tackle stigma: The INDIGO Network The INDIGO Network runs an evidence-based research and implementation programme to understand the mechanisms and consequences of stigma and discrimination in more than 40 countries and to develop and test new ways to end stigma. Several LMICs participate in the network, including Bangladesh, Brazil, China, Egypt, Ethiopia, India, Jamaica, Malaysia, Nepal, Nigeria, South Africa, Tunisia, Türkiye and Venezuela.
