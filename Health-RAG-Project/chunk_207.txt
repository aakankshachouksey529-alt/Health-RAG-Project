In case of water collection, the concerned authority must be contacted.  Areas where water has collected should be filled with mud. Area around wells, water taps and hand pumps should be cemented with proper drainage to prevent water collection.  There is usually a community dustbin built in each locality. The clearing and cleaning is the responsibility of the village council or municipality. The community should make sure that it is done regularly. Bleaching powder can be spread in and around the dustbin.
