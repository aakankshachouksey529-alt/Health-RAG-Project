vii Executive summary Background Non-communicable diseases (NCDs) are the leading cause of mortality in the world. Modifiable risk factors such as unhealthy diets, physical inactivity, tobacco use and harmful use of alcohol are major risk factors. Obesity is also a risk factor for diet-related NCDs and is linked to millions of deaths globally.
