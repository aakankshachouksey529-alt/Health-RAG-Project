BOX 5.6 VISHRAM: a community initiative to reduce suicides in rural India The Vidarbha Stress and Health Program (VISHRAM) was a community-based programme run over 18 months in 2014 and 2015. It was designed to address the mental health risk factors for suicide in the rural Amravati district of Vidarbha, central India.
