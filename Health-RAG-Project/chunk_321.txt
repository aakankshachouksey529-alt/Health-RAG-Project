78 Cancer of the Mouth Cancer of the mouth is caused by chewing tobacco mixed with other substances such as betel nut, areca, and lime. Not only is it chewed but it is also kept in the side of the mouth. Cancer is seen on the side where the tobacco is kept and chewed. Signs and symptoms of cancer of the mouth The first stage is a whitish patch seen in the mouth. This can be easily seen and can be treated and cured.
