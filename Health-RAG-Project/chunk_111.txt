28 Adolescence: 10 - 19 years Adolescence is the age between 10 to 19 years. This is the time when changes occur in both girls and boys. Body changes occur due to increase in hormone (secretions of certain glands) levels in the blood, which results in body development, sexual development and emotional changes.
