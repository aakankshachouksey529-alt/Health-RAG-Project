Recommendations 4 and 5 are based on evidence of moderate certainty overall from a systematic review of randomized controlled trials and prospective observational studies conducted in adults that assessed higher compared with lower intakes of dietary fibre (34). This systematic review found that higher intakes of dietary fibre led to favourable improvements in obesity and NCDs risk factors, and were associated with reduced risk of all-cause mortality, CVDs, stroke, coronary heart disease, type 2 diabetes
