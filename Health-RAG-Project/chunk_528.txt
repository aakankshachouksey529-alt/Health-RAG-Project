131 Yogic breathing (Pranayama) It is an important component of Yogic practiceIt involves slow, deep inhalation, holding of breath and slow deep complete exhalation. The main objective of Yogic breathing is to gain control over the autonomic nervous system thereby regulating the physiological as well as mental functions of the body.
