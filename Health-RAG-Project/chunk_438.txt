109  For the management of any ailment or disease, as much care as possible should be initially given at home.  It is most important not to waste time. Management of an ailment or the symptoms should be started as soon as possible at home.  If the condition does not improve with initial care or gets worse, the patient needs the attention of the health worker or doctor.  When in doubt, it is better to consult the health worker at the earliest. Advice given by the health worker and doctor must be followed.
