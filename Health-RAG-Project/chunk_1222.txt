EVIDENCE BOX 4.3 Children in institutions An estimated 5.4 million children live in institutional care around the world, many of whom have mental health conditions. Many institutions for children are unregistered, making monitoring almost impossible. They often provide environments that are neglectful and more abusive than they are caring.
