There were no adverse effects on any outcome assessed in RCTs, but increased risks of endometrial cancer with higher dietary fibre intake and of prostate cancer with higher whole grain intake were observed in prospective cohort studies. The certainty in the evidence for these two outcomes was very low and low, respectively, and there are no clear biological mechanisms that would explain these potential relationships.
