CASE STUDY BOX 5.7 Peru: innovative financing for sustainable improvement Since 2013, a process of mental health reform in Peru has been closing the care gap for people in need. The reform is focused on shifting care for mental health conditions from specialized psychiatric hospitals to community-based care. Analysis suggests that the reform has supported a gradual but significant expansion in mental health services: in 2009, 9% of people who needed care were covered by 2018 this figure had risen to 26%.
