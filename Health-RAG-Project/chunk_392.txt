It is important to inform the health authorities about suspected cases of cholera to enable them to identify the source of infection (e.g. contaminated water supply) and take appropriate action so that the disease does not spread further.  One of the most important means by which cholera can be prevented is ensuring supply of safe, clean water. Water supplied by municipal authorities is generally safe.
