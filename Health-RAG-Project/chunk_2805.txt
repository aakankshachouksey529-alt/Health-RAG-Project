26 Carbohydrate intake for adults and children: WHO guideline  The recommendations covering vegetable and fruit intake are not limited to fresh vegetables and fruits. Evidence from the systematic reviews suggests health benefits from a wide range of vegetables and fruits, including those that are fresh, cooked, frozen or canned.
