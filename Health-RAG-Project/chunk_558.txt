Teaching/Learning methods There are many methods to help people learn. These include lectures or presentation, demonstration, group discussion, group work, role play, etc. This section will explain some of these methods that assist in educating and empowering people. Lectures and presentation Presentation is the most used teaching method. It is often referred to as the lecture method, presentation or chalk and talk.
