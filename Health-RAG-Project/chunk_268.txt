Using mosquito nets at night. If available, mosquito nets that have long- lasting insecticides on them should be used. This type of mosquito net should not be washed too often.  At night, the body should be covered with a thin sheet. One can wear thin cotton clothes to cover the arms and legs. Children must wear thin protective clothing at night.
