Deinstitutionalization is not the same as discharging people from institutions as it must be accompanied by sufficient financial, structural and strategic investment in community-based services. This is essential to meet the health and social needs of former institutional residents, and their families or carers, and also to prevent new- or re-admissions (see Fig. 7.3).
