41 Water hygiene Unclean water and food have germs, which enter the body and lead to a number of diseases. Some of these are: diarrhoea, typhoid fever, cholera, hepatitis A (jaundice), food poisoning or intestinal worms. It is important to pay attention to water and food hygiene to prevent sickness.. Water that is used for drinking and cooking purposes must be safe from both germs and chemicals (arsenic and fluoride which is common in Bangladesh, India, Myanmar, Nepal, Thailand).
