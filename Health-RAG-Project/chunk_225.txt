54 What to do in case of a poisonous snake or cobra bite  Since the poison can spread through the blood, the person should not be moved.  The body part, which was bitten, should be kept in a lower position than the heart.  A tight cloth or bandage can be tied tightly just above the bite to slow down the blood flow towards the heart.
