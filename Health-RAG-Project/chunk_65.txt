Choosing foods from all the food groups each day and choosing a variety of foods within each food group will help the body meet its nutritional requirements. No single food can provide all the nutrients needed to maintain good health. How is diet related to health? Good health requires a balance of food, with adequate amounts of cereals, carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, minerals and physical activity.
