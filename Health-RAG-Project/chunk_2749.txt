Low vegetable and fruit intake in LMICs are of particular concern: recent estimates suggest that less than 2030% of individuals in many LMICs meet WHO recommendations for vegetable and fruit intake (29, 30). Although the reasons underlying the variability in intakes of dietary fibre, whole grains, vegetables, fruits and pulses are complex and varied across different settings, common issues are supply, access and availability, and individual behaviour and preferences.
