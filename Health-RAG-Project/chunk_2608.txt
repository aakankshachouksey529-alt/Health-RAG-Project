Scope This guideline is part of the larger effort to update the population nutrient intake goals for the prevention of NCDs established by the 2002 Joint WHO/FAO Expert Consultation on Diet, Nutrition and the Prevention of Chronic Diseases (32). It is intended to complement other WHO guidance on healthy diets, particularly the WHO guideline on free sugars intake (15).
