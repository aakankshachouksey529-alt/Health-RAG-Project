49 To prevent the breeding of mosquitoes inside or outside, the following steps could be taken: a. All empty pots, utensils, empty tins, car or tractor tyres and anything in which water can collect, should be removed. b. Empty flower pots should be kept upside down. c. Water tanks and water storage utensils should be covered. d. Once a week, on a fixed day, room coolers should be emptied, the water tank dried and filled with fresh water.
