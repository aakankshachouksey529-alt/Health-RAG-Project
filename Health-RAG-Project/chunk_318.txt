77 Those who have lung cancer can have:  Cough with sputum.  Blood in the sputum.  Fever.  Chest pain.  Weight loss. The person must go to hospital or health centre for investigations. If the cancer has already spread, the person may have:  Breathlessness.  Difficulty in speaking.  Lumps in the neck.  Hoarseness of voice.
