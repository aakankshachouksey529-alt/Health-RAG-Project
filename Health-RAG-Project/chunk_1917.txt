CASE STUDY BOX 7.3 Trieste, Italy: 40 years of community-based mental health care In Trieste, Italy, community-based mental health care has proven effective for more than 40 years. The Trieste transformation began in the 1970s, with the relocation of people living in the citys asylum to the community. Funds previously used to run the asylum were gradually diverted to develop a comprehensive network of community-based services.
