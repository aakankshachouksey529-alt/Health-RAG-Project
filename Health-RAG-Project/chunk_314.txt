76 Cancer of the cervix Cancer of the cervix is a common cancer in women in the region. In the earliest stage, the woman may not have any complaints, but if it is left untreated, the cancer will progress to the next stage when women will have vaginal discharge, low back pain, and off and on bleeding. If not treated, the cancer will enter the final stage when the cancer spreads to the nearby areas or to distant parts of the body.
