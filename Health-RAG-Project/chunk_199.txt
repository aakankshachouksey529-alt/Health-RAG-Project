48 Toilets. Most houses in urban areas have a toilet attached to the house. In the villages, there may not be a toilet and the people use the fields. This can lead to worm infections when people go barefoot to the fields. a. It is best to construct a toilet in the house. There are many types of toilets which can be made, such as flush latrine, water-sealed latrine, pit latrine, etc. The local health staff or related government agency can be contacted for advice. b. The toilet should be kept clean. Adequate
