106 If the pet dog has not been vaccinated or has been partially vaccinated, then the treatment may be the same as for stray dog bite. The doctor will decide this and his advice should be followed. Snake bite In the case of a snake bite:  A tight bandage should be tied above the level of the bite, if it is on the arm or leg. This stops the poison from spreading to other parts of the body.
