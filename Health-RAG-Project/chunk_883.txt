Protective factors at these levels include positive family interactions, quality education, decent work conditions, safe neighbour- hoods, community cohesion and shared cultural meaning and identity (52). Nurturing and supportive parenting can help protect people against developing mental health conditions (18). Supportive families and carers are important at any age and can be real enablers of recovery for people living with mental health conditions (read Elenis experience in Chapter 7).
