Bore Wells: The surroundings of the well should be clean, paved with bricks to prevent water collection outside the well. Wells should be covered and protected from animals. People should not be allowed to wash clothes and utensils close to the well. This is because the dirty water seeps into the ground next to the well wall and contaminates the well water.
