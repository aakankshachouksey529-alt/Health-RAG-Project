12  It would be impossible for us to do anything without our muscles. Absolutely everything that we conceive of with our brain is expressed as muscular motion. The only ways for a person to express an idea is with the muscles of the larynx, mouth and tongue (spoken words), with the muscles of the fingers (writing or the expressive movement of the hands or with the skeletal muscles (body language, dancing, running, building or fighting, to name a few).
