70 Diabetes in pregnancy can cause complications to the mother and the infant:  The mother can get infections.  The baby can be big and the delivery difficult.  After delivery, the baby can have low blood sugar levels or breathing difficulties. It is important that all pregnant women should attend an ante-natal clinic during their pregnancy and give birth in a hospital.
