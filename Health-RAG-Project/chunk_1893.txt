It is more than just a process that needs to be well-managed. It requires transforming the lives and mindsets of all involved. Responsible deinstitutionalization puts into operation a shared vision to humanize mental health care, based on understanding the importance of community, liberty and autonomy to all people. Deinstitutionalization is not the same as discharging people from institutions. Progressively reducing caseloads to close or repurpose long-stay psychiatric institutions is important.
