NARRATIVE Navigating the criminal justice system with a mental health condition Lauras experience In the Spring of 2015, there were eight warrants out for my arrest. My crimes were misdemeanours, and several warrants were compounded charges resulting from a failure to appear in court. I was petrified to go to court alone and equally scared to not show up. I could barely keep the time of day. Eventually I was hospitalized. On discharge, I returned to a shelter and began to get help. I retained an attorney.
