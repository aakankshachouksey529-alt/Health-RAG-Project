First Aid. It can:  Prevent the condition of the injured or sick person from getting worse  Save a life  Helps to keep the person comfortable till proper medical help is given However, it is important to know when to give first aid and when not to. For example, one should give first aid in case of a small injury, such as a burn, a cut, a scratch or bruise. But if the persons condition looks serious, there is lot of bleeding or pain, or if the injury seems to be inside the body, it is best to get medical
