142 Group work Group work is one of the group-based learning methods. Participants are divided into small groups of 6  8 persons to exchange their views. Situations or problems may be given to them. Their tasks involve discussing the situation/problem and coming up with solutions or recommendations. This exercise helps in improving the communication and analytical skills of the participants and brings out new ideas.
