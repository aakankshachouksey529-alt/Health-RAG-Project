If possible, we must try to segregate waste into different categories   Biodegradable (kitchen and garden waste)  Those that can be recycled (PET bottles, plastic containers, glasses, plastic bags etc),  Recyclable and hazardous wastes (mobile phones, computers, batteries, printers, light bulbs, medicines etc). Biodegradable wastes can be composted in a pit to produce manure if one has sufficient space, otherwise it can be given to the waste
