Importantly, the plan includes a wide range of implementation options for realizing each objective, recognizing that their relevance to different countries will depend on national circumstances. In 2019, WHO Member States reaffirmed their commitment to the plans original four objectives and extended the timeline for achieving them to 2030 (from 2020).
