When the mosquito bites another person, the germ is transmitted into his/her blood. This mosquito bites at night and, depending on the type, it breeds either in slow flowing streams, irrigation canals and seepages, well water, overhead tanks, ponds, drains, collections of water on the roadside and near houses.
