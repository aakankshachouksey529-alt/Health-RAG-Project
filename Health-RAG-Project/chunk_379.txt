One should make sure the patient continues to eat food and takes plenty of home-available fluids including plain water to prevent dehydration. Other fluids such as coconut water, rice water or lemonade can also be given. Infants should continue to be breastfed. WHO-ORS is a good fluid to prevent and treat dehydration.
