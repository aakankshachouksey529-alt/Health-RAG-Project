Ultimately, interventions provided under UHC should ensure that all essential mental health needs are covered. This means they need to be comprehensive, including evidence-based psychosocial and pharmacological interventions, as described in WHOs UHC compendium (see section 5.1.3 Evidence to inform policy and practice) (179).
