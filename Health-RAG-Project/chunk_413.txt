102 Observing careful habits to prevent burns is most important (see chapter 3 for details). Injuries Cuts The important point in dealing with cuts is to try and stop the bleeding or get medical help as soon as possible if the bleeding is excessive. The cloth used as bandage for the cut has to be clean. A dirty cloth should not be used under any circumstances.
