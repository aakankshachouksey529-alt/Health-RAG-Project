Germs that cause coughs and colds spread in crowded places. Wherever possible, avoid crowded places to help reduce exposure to disease-causing agents. Indoor air pollution, for example, due to use of coal or wood for cooking indoors as well as indoor smoking of tobacco products increases risk of respiratory infections.
