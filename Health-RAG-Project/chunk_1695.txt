Any regulation aiming to reduce access to means of suicide should first focus on the most common and most lethal methods used. This varies from country to country, as well as within countries and across socio-demographic groups. Highly hazardous pesticides Pesticide self-poisonings account for up to one fifth of all suicides globally and are of particular concern in LMICs, where many people live in rural, agricultural communities with easy access to highly hazardous pesticides.
