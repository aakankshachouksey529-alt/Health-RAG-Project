82 Fever A healthy body maintains its temperature within a narrow range of 37.00C and 37.70C. Fever means there is an increase in the normal temperature. During common ailments such as cold, a person may develop mild fever. This is not a cause for worry as the fever usually goes away as the cold gets better.
