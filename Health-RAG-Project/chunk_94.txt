A bath is not necessary for the baby for the first two days as it washes away the protective coating on the skin. Subsequently, the baby may be bathed daily. Eyes, ears and private parts should be cleaned during the bath. The mouth should also be cleaned after every feed.  The umbilical stump must be cleaned and kept dry. Any oil/ medicine should not be applied. If there is discharge from the umbilical cord, it is important to CONTACT THE HEALTH WORKER.  The baby must be immunized immediately after birth
