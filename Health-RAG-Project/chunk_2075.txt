7.17 Headspace: no wrong door for young people in Australia and Denmark Headspace started in 2006 in Australia, to address the gap in mental health services for young people aged 1225 years. There are now more than 100 Headspace centres in Australia and 28 in Denmark. Local young people help design each centre and the services it provides. And each centre is deliberately located near public transport hubs for easy access. Mental health professionals at the centres provide evidence-based psychological care
