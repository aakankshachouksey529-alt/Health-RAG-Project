Prevalence in older adults Around 13% of adults aged 70 years and over lived with a mental disorder in 2019, mainly depressive and anxiety disorders. Sex differences in rates of mental disorders increase in this age category as 14.2% of women and 11.7% of men aged over 70 years are estimated to have a mental disorder.
