Infection in any part of the body can also cause fever as in the case of sore throat, bronchitis, pneumonia, urinary tract infection, and so on. Diseases transmitted by mosquitoes, such as malaria, dengue, chikungunya, also manifest as fever. Fever can also be caused by other infections, allergies or even some cancers.
