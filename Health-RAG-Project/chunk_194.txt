47 ENVIRONMENTAL HEALTH The environment to be discussed here includes the environment inside the house, the nearby surroundings, and the community in which people live. Household and community environment have great effects on human health. Garbage thrown in open areas, wastewater and rainwater collected here and there creates an unhealthy environment. Such places are good breeding zones for flies and mosquitoes, which bring germs and cause serious diseases such as malaria, dengue fever and diarrhoea,
