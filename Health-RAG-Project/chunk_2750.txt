Detailed discussion of these themes is beyond the scope of this guideline however, they are summarized below. Supply. For most or all individuals to achieve the dietary goals in this guideline, a stable and consistent supply of whole grains, vegetables, fruits and pulses will be necessary. Supply issues currently exist in some settings, particularly for fresh vegetables and fruits (62, 63), which are generally more perishable than grains and pulses, and are thus subject to spoilage and waste during storage
