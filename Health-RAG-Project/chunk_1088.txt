3.3.4 The services gap Poor treatment coverage With nearly a billion people in the world living with a mental disorder, the need for adequate and accessible services is evident. But all over the globe, mental health systems are failing to meet their populations needs. The gap between prevalence and treatment remains unacceptably large.
