The four greatest risk factors for NCDs  tobacco use, unhealthy diets, physical inactivity and harmful use of alcohol  are all linked with various mental health conditions (162). Childhood adversity, which is a major risk factor for later-life mental health conditions, is similarly related to a range of adult-onset NCDs, including heart disease, diabetes and asthma (163).
