At older ages, mental health continues to be shaped by physical, social and environmental conditions as well as the cumulative impacts of earlier life experiences and specific stressors related to ageing. For example, loss of functional ability, musculoskeletal pain, bereavement and isolation can all result in loneliness and psychological distress.
