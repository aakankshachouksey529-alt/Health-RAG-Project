Food should always be covered and protected from flies.  The cooking surface should be kept clean before and after use. Preparation of food It is best to prepare fresh food everyday. Leftover food should be kept covered in a cool, dry and clean place. If available, a refrigerator can be used to store leftover food for the next day.
