Starting in 2010, in partnership with the Carter Centers Mental Health Program, Liberia has trained 306 primary health workers (registered nurses, physician assistants and midwives) in mental health through a six-month training programme. These include 166 specialists in general mental health care and 140 specialists in child and adolescent mental health care. with a focus on practical application.
