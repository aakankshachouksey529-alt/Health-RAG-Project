57 What to do during a Tsunami  Community members should listen to the radio and TV to learn if there is a tsunami warning when an earthquake occurs in or near a coastal area.  They should move inland to higher ground immediately and stay there.  If there is noticeable recession in water away from the shoreline, this is natures tsunami warning and should be heeded. Members should move away immediately.  It is wise to stay away from flooded and damaged areas until officials say it is safe to return. Debris
