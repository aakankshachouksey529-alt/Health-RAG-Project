NARRATIVE 3.1.3 Burden Burden of disease studies estimate the population-wide impact of living with disease and injury and dying prematurely. They involve calculations using the Disability-Adjusted Life Year (DALY), where one DALY represents the loss of one year of full health. DALYs combine in one measure the years of life lost to premature mortality (YLLs) and years of healthy life lost to disability (YLDs) to estimate the overall burden from each cause of disease and injury.
