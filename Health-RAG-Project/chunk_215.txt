Young children and elderly people can slip and fall down stairs. Stairs should have a railing to hold on to, children and the elderly should be helped when they are walking up or down the stairs. Injuries from sharp objects: Knives and sharp objects can cause serious cuts and injuries. These objects must be kept in safe places away from children. Adults should use them carefully.
