104 Accidents  Any accident leading to serious injury and bleeding has to be treated at the district hospital. One must not delay or waste time the victim must be taken to the hospital as soon as possible.  Bleeding areas have to be bandaged tightly with clean cloth as already described.  Broken bones have to be managed as described.  An accident victim should be covered with a thick cloth or blanket if she/he is bleeding heavily.
