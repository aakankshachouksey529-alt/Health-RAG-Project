CASE STUDY BOX 7.21 Home Again: group homes for women in India Home Again is a housing intervention with supportive services for homeless women in India living with long-term mental health conditions. Run by nongovernmental organizations The Banyan, Home Again provides shared housing in urban and rural spaces where four to five women live together in home-like environments, with a mix of work, leisure and social activities that are facilitated by a multidisciplinary team of service providers.
