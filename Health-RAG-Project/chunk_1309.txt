WHOs menu of costeffective interventions for mental health identifies a selection of mental health interventions for which costeffectiveness information is available (222). At a population level, the menu identifies universal and indicated school-based social and emotional learning programmes and regulatory bans on highly hazardous pesticides (which are commonly used in suicides in several LMICs) as costeffective and affordable
