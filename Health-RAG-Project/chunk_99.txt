Milk should be boiled and fed with a clean cup and spoon. Leftover milk should NOT be given to the infant. Feeding with a bottle is also not advisable as the bottle may not be properly cleaned. Water should not be added to the milk.  Cleanliness during cooking, before feeding and after cleaning the infants stools is to be maintained.
