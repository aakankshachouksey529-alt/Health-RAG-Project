Mental health stressors The COVID-19 pandemic has created several short- or long-term stressors for mental health (63). Stress from the potential health impacts of the virus. For some people, and especially during the early months  when little was known about the virus and there were strict public health and social measures  the fear of infection and death (both for oneself and for loved ones) was distressing (read Esenams experience).
