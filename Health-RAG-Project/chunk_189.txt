Fruit should be freshly cut and eaten. If cut fruit is to be consumed later, it should be covered properly to protect it from dust, flies and other contaminants. Storing foods Storing food in the wrong place or at the wrong temperature can lead to the growth of germs.  Cooked foods should be kept covered until they are served to protect them from dust, flies and contaminants.  Meat and meat products should be consumed fresh.
