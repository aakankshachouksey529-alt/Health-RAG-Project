7 Chapter 1  Health and Self care Health The World Health Organisation defines Health as a state of complete physical, mental and social well being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity. This means that a person is considered healthy when she/he is:  Physically fit  Mentally stable  Able to function peacefully and effectively in society  Able to perform daily functions at home and at work.
