Mental health is critically important for everyone, everywhere. It is an inherent and vital part of our overall health and well-being and affects our lives in many ways (see Chapter 2 Principles and drivers in public mental health). Our mental health enables us to function and thrive as individuals, family members and community participants. It helps us cope with stress and adapt to change.
