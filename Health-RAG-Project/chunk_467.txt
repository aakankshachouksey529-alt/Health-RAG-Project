117  A healthy mind is as important as a healthy body. Positive practices such as praying, chanting, giving charity and assisting others enhances the state of mind Suppressible urges There are three types of urges, expression of which create problems. They are concerned with mind, body and speech. Urges of the mind that need to be suppressed are: Greed, grief, fear, anger, pride, shamelessness, jealousy, attachment, desires, hatred, etc. Urges concerned with speech are: Telling lies, rudeness, back-biting
