Hand washing is VERY IMPORTANT. The method to be followed for proper hand washing is:  Adequate water. Running water from tap is best. If water is stored in a utensil, the quantity MUST be adequate.  Use soap.  The hands should be rubbed well. It is important to clean the nails, between the fingers and on top of the hands. Soap should be washed off well and the hands dried with a clean cloth.  When do we HAVE to wash hands?  After using the toilet  When handling foods, before and after eating.  After
