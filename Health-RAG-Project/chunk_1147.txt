This massive gap between the need for and uptake of care was the primary motivation behind WHOs Mental Health Gap Action Programme (mhGAP). The programme seeks to significantly expand coverage of evidence-based interventions for a range of priority conditions, with a focus on primary health care and other non-specialized health care settings in LMICs (161).
