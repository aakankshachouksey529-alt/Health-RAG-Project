8.3 Combining efforts for change No one should travel the paths to transformation alone. It will take the combined efforts of all of us, across all sectors, to transform mental health and mental health care. Everyone has a part to play. Individuals can make a big difference by better understanding and valuing mental health  both their own and that of others  and taking action to promote and protect it in daily life.
