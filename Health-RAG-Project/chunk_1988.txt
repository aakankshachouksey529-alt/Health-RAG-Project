By 2021, nearly 2 600 doctors had been trained across the 29 (out of 81) provinces where large numbers of refugees live. That means around 8% of Turkish general practitioners working in primary care settings (2 100 people) and more than 50% of Syrian doctors (561 people) have now been trained using mhGAP.
