Solar disinfection: Exposing water in disposable, clear plastic bottles to sunlight on the roof of a house or any raised flat platform for 8 hours during a sunny day, or for two days during cloudy days can render germs as inactive. Water should be consumed directly or transferred to a clean glass. To be effective, solar disinfection must be applied to clear water.
