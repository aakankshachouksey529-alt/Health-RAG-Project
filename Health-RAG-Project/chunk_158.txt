38 Mouth and Teeth: Clean mouth and teeth are important for health and for social reasons. Unclean mouth and teeth can give you bad breath. The teeth must be kept clean and gums massaged. This is important as the gums support the teeth. Hygiene of the mouth has to be maintained right from birth. Infants and children  The mouth, tongue and gums must be gently wiped at least 2-3 times a day, using a finger wrapped with a soft, clean, damp cloth. This will prevent formation of white deposit seen on the tongue
