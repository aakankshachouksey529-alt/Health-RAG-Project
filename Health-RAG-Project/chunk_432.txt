107 Ear Ache Pain in the ears occurs commonly in children. The common causes are:  Cough and cold: The tube which connects the nose and the ear gets blocked when the child has a severe cold with blocked nose. He/she will complain of pain in the ears. A smaller infant may cry excessively and touch the ear. Sometimes pus comes out of the ear, which means that the child has an ear infection.  Wax in the ear may also cause ear ache.
