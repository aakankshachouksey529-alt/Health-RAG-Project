Integrating mental health services into primary care is a key strategy at the level of health care organizations (see section 7.2.1 Mental health in primary care). Collaborative care models in particular have been shown to improve mental health outcomes, including in people with comorbid NCDs (176, 177). A proven approach is to use service delivery platforms that already exist for chronic diseases as the basis for expanding mental health services.
