84 Fluid and food must be given during fever. The patient should be given plenty of liquids such as plain water, lemonade, tea, coconut water, rice water or other commonly available fluids at home. He/she should be fed with easily digested food during the fever such as porridge, curd rice, biscuits, fruit, toast, tea, fresh juice, milk, thin soups and anything else that the person may want.
