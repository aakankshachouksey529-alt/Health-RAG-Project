Our mental health differs greatly depending on the circumstances in which we are born, raised and live our lives (26). This is because mental health is determined by a complex interplay of individual, family, community and structural factors that vary over time and space and that are experienced differently from person to person (27). Mental health conditions result from the interaction between an individu- als vulnerability and the stress caused by life events and chronic stressors (see Fig. 2.3) (28).
