BOX 7.7 Step-by-Step: guided self-help for depression in Lebanon Step-by-Step is a new WHO digital self-help intervention. It provides psychoeducation through a narrated story and uses interactive exercises to teach people to use therapeutic techniques to reduce their depression. It focuses on behavioural activation combined with stress management (slow breathing), increasing social support and relapse prevention.
