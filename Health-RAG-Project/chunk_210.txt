The common accidents are:  Fire and Burns  Poisoning  Suffocation  Drowning  Falling from a height/stairs  Slipping on a wet, uneven floor  Injuries from sharp objects Fire and burns:  Children can burn themselves when they play with a match box, or a lighted candle or if there is a utensil with hot water on the ground. All such articles should be kept out of reach of children.  Women should wear fitting clothes and be careful while cooking or handling hot liquids. Poisoning: Common poisons in households
