CASE STUDY BOX 5.2 WHO Special Initiative for Mental Health In 2019, the WHO Special Initiative for Mental Health was established to accelerate access to mental health services through UHC. The goal is to ensure 100 million more people have access to quality and affordable mental health care. The initiative will initially be implemented in 12 countries to demonstrate what is possible. Eight countries have already been selected: Bangladesh, Ghana, Jordan, Nepal, Paraguay, Philippines, Ukraine and Zimbabwe.
