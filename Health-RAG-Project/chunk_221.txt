What to do in case of sprains Sometimes, joints such as the ankle or wrist can get twisted. The joint gets swollen and may be quite painful. Children may sprain themselves while playing.  Ice should be applied immediately, if it is available, to reduce the swelling.  The joint should not be moved if possible, till medical help is reached.  A
