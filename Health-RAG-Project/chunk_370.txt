92 Cough and cold Everyone has a cold sometime or the other. Most of these are caused by viral infections and usually start with:  Sneezing  Runny nose  Irritation in the throat and cough There may also be:  Low-grade fever  Body-ache, headache  General feeling of being unwell, which lasts for 5-7 days Anyone can be affected by cough and cold at some time or the other, but it commonly occurs in:  Young children.  School-going children.  Elderly people.  People with asthma and lung diseases.  Chronic
