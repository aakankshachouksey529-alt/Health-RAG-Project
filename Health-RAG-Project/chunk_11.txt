8 The Human Body There are essentially nine systems that are responsible for the structure and functions in the human body. The Circulatory and Respiratory Systems make oxygen and nutrients available to the entire body. The Digestive and Excretory Systems are responsible for making the nutrients available as well as for expelling out unwanted waste from the body.
