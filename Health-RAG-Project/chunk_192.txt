Food items must be taken out of the tin and transferred to a suitable airtight container instead.  If cooked leftovers need to be kept in the fridge, they must be allowed to cool to room temperature. Any leftovers must be used within two days.  Any food that has passed its use-by (expiry) date MUST not be used. Information about the date of manufacture and expiry is important.
