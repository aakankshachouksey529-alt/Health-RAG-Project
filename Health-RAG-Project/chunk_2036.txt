Community mental health centres may cater for a range of mental health conditions in adults, children and adolescents alike. They look different in different countries but often have a multi-faceted role in supporting people (see Fig. 7.4). In community mental health centres people usually receive services for acute episodes or relapses in functioning, and may be admitted for short periods.
