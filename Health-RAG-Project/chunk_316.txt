Women aged 35 years and over, especially those who have family history of breast cancer, must see medical doctor or health professionals at least once a year for medical check up and tests for breast cancer and cervical cancer.  Those who have any early signs and symptoms of breast cancer or cervical cancer must see a medical doctor or health professional immediately. It is important not to delay.
