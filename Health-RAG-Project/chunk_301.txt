Chronic Respiratory Diseases Bronchial Asthma and Chronic Obstructive Lung Disease are long-lasting respiratory diseases which cause breathing difficulty. Bronchial Asthma is a disease where the person is allergic to or cannot tolerate certain things. Sometimes, it is very difficult to find out exactly what the person is allergic to.
