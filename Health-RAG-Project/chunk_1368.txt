Psychotropic medicines can, where appropriate, be used to reduce the symptoms of priority mental health conditions and improve functioning. Psychotropic medicines on the WHO model list of essential medicines include medicines for psychosis, bipolar disorder, anxiety disorders, depression and obsessive-compulsive disorder (243).
