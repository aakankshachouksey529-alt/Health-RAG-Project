23 Pregnancy and Childbirth  By the age of 20, girls and boys are usually physically and mentally mature for marriage. Each country in the region has a legal age for marriage. It is best to follow the rule.  After marriage, before planning a baby, it is advisable for the couple to get a health check up done to ensure both are in good health, with no underlying health concerns.
