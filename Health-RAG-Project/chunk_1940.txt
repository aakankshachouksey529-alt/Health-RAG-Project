CASE STUDY BOX 7.5 Friendship benches for mental health In Zimbabwe, the Friendship Bench project has been integrating mental health into other health programmes for more than 20 years. The project uses problem-solving therapies delivered by community volunteers, known as grandmothers, to address kufungisisa (which means thinking too much and is a local concept in the Shona language that denotes a range of non-psychotic mental health conditions).
