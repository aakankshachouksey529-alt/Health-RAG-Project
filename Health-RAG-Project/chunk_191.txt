46 If a refrigerator is available:  Fresh and frozen food must be stored in the refrigerator or freezer as soon as possible after it is bought from the shop. This is especially important if the weather is hot.  Raw meat should be stored in an airtight container at the bottom of the refrigerator. This is important to prevent the juices or blood dripping onto other food kept in the fridge.  Tins of food that have been opened should not be kept in the fridge.
