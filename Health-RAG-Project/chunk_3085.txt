Supply issues currently exist in some settings, particularly for fresh vegetables and fruits (55, 56), which are generally more perishable than grains and pulses, and are thus subject to spoilage and waste during storage and transport. Access and availability. Even if sufficient quantities of whole grains, vegetables, fruits and pulses are produced, large- scale achievement of the dietary goals in this guideline will be difficult if individuals cannot afford them or otherwise cannot obtain them.
