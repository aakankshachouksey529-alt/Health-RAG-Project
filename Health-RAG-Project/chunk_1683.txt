NARRATIVE Receiving help guided me to inner strength and self-awareness Enochs experience The pain would not stop. In my head, in my mind, in my heart. I did not want to live anymore. I was about to end my life right there and then, and I was moments away from dying. But I survived. My boyfriend had come into the room in that split second and stopped me from what I was about to do. I started sobbing and wailed in rage. I was disappointed in myself that I could not kill myself and felt worse than a failure.
