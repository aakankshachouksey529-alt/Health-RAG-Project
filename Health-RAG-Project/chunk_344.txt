Measles occurs in children who have not been given measles vaccine.  Early symptoms begin with running nose, watering eyes, fever.  After 3-4 days, red spots appear on the face and forehead and slowly spread down to the rest of the body.  The fever goes down once the rash covers the body and the child feels better.
