THE HEALTH WORKER/ HEALTH CENTRE OR HOSPITAL SHOULD BE CONTACTED : If infants and young children:  experience fast and difficult breathing.  have wheezing (whistling sound while breathing).  refuse to eat or stop drinking milk.  have ear ache.  cry excessively If the elderly:  cough out sputum that changes colour.  experience rapid breathing or have difficulty
