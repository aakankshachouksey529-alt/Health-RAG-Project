WHO suggests the following intakes of naturally occurring dietary fibre as consumed in foods (conditional recommendation):  25 years old, at least 15 g per day  69 years old, at least 21 g per day  10 years or older, at least 25 g per day. Rationale for recommendation 1  Recommendation 1 is based on evidence from seven systematic reviews that assessed the effects of higher compared with lower intakes of whole grains, vegetables and fruits, or pulses.
