WAO provided more than 7 100 consultations via phone, SMS and email. MHPSS hotline. In partnership with Mercy Malaysia, the Ministry of Health established the MHPSS hotline to support people coping with the impacts of COVID-19. It is staffed by a mix of ministry counsellors and trained lay volunteers. From January to June 2021, the hotline received more than 122 300 calls asking for support. Around 90% of these were related to social problems such as unemployment, loss of income, and family-related issues.
