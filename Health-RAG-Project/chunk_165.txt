40 Sleeping: Ideally, 6-8 hours of sleep a day is necessary for adequate rest in adults and proper growth in children. While sleeping, it is important to avoid mosquito and insect bites. Bedrooms should be screened to prevent insects and mosquitoes. If the room is not screened, one should sleep under a mosquito net.
