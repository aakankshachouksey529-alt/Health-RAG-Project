NARRATIVE Twelve hospital admissions only one was my choice Sandras experience Pinned down by policemen and paramedics. Gagged and handcuffed like a criminal as I was knocked unconscious by a medical cocktail. Waking up not knowing where I am, clothes off in what feels like a prison. My crime? At worst, being loud, antagonistic and manic. My life sentence? Bipolar disorder. This was the first of my eleven involuntary hospital admissions. I was literally stripped of any semblance of human rights or dignity.
