The threshold of at least 25 g per day was selected based on the doseresponse relationship seen in the observational studies between dietary fibre intake and reduced risk for several NCD and mortality outcomes. This relationship was observed at intakes up to 40 g per day, but the number of studies reporting data began to taper off at 30 g or more per day.
