115 Seasonal guidelines Season Diet regimen Activities regimen Spring (Mar - Apr) Light, dry, bitter, hot  astringent foods such as honey, wheat, barley are best. Heavy, oily foods and sweet, sour tastes should be reduced/ avoided. Moderate exercise, oil and powder massage, fomentations, steam, use of lukewarm water for bath and drinking. Avoid day sleeping. Summer (May-Jun.) Sweet, cold, oily, light, liquid food items, seasonal fruits, milk, rice, barley, sweets, butter milk, coconut water, cold drinks
